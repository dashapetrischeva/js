Task 1

Describe an array of objects – websites developed by the company with the following properties 

----- Properties ------					
	- company name at the time of development (the name is changed periodically)
	- company owner
	- sponsors (array of sponsors)					
			* sponsor's last name
			* sponsor's first name
			* sponsor's contribution amount				
	- year of release
	- site cost

Find:

1) total cost of all websites

2) number of websites developed between 2000 and 2009

3) number of websites where the total sponsor contributions were greater than 100000

4) create a comprehensive list of all sponsors (while they may repeat, simply gather them into an array)

5) find the year when the profit was the highest

6) sort the list in descending order of profit

7) create 2 separate lists with copies of objects that contain websites with cost less than 10000 and more than 10000
