Homework 19

  • Task 1

    Solution

    Given 10 lines of text "Hello!" in separate divs. When clicking on any of them, all subsequent ones should be colored in red.

  • Task 2

    Solution

    Given 5 input elements. When entering a value in any of them, the others should be automatically filled in descending order (each previous one has a value 1 less than the next), and all subsequent ones 1 more (each subsequent one has a value 1 more than the previous)

  • Task 6

    Solution

    Given a list of athletes. Need to form a list of those who will participate in the competition. In this case, there are two columns. In one, all athletes are displayed, in the other – the list of those who were selected. When clicking on the green arrow, the athlete moves to the competition list. When clicking on the red arrow, the athlete moves to the general list.
