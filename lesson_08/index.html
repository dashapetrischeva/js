Homework 8

  • Task 1

    Solution

    Given an array containing grades for K subjects, find the average grade and determine which category it belongs to (excellent student, poor student (has at least one failing grade), good student (grades are good and excellent), or fair student (has at least one fair grade)).

  • Task 2

    Solution

    Given an array containing the number of customers visiting a store throughout a week, display on the screen:

    ● numbers of days when the number of customers was less than 20;

    ● numbers of days when the number of customers was minimum;

    ● numbers of days when the number of customers was minimum;

    ● total number of customers on working days and separately the total number of days on weekends.

  • Task 3

    Solution

    Given a sequence of student grades, calculate the number of:

    1) fives

    2) good grades (good, excellent);

    3) grades that are below the average.

  • Task 4

    Solution

    Given a sequence of payments throughout the year, find the total amount of money for:

    1) the entire year;

    2) the first half of the year;

    3) the second half of the year;

    4) summer;

    5) the second quarter;

    6) even months (with even numbers);

    7) months that are initial in the season (spring, summer, fall, winter).

  • Task 5

    Solution

    Given a one-dimensional array containing a certain winning amount (elements are filled with random values from -500 to 500), provide the user with the opportunity to select element numbers (until they decline). Calculate the total winnings.
